About Olivia

Olivia trained at Rubicon dance in Cardiff and then the Northern School of Contemporary Dance in Leeds. Upon graduating, she danced in Amsterdam with de Kiss Moves fusion company and also in Guangzhou, China where she was Dance Captain.

After returning to the UK, Olivia performed in (In)visible Dancing with Protein Dance, My Super Sweet Sixteen with MTV and was in the Bodhi Culture music video.
Olivia moved to London in 2014 where she became a member of Boy Blue Entertainment and Boadicea (Bsquad).
She has since performed at the Barbican- A Night with a Boy Blue, Latitude Festival, SKIN tour with Company 201, Sadler's Wells- Breakin Convention, BoShow, World of Dance PA, OpenLab, Stratford Royal Theatre showcase, Dive In festival, Harbour Music festival, Halo Lighting event, Dancer's Delight, Breakout, Women in Sport.

A passionate choreographer, Olivia was the Assistant Choreographer for The Licensing Award Ceremony with Warner Brothers, Principle choreographer whilst on tour in Guangzhou and has choreographed for IMD Legion- Britain's Got Talent (ITV), Jump Off TV, Breakin' Convention, Avant Garde Youth, National Theatre Wales (supported artist), Black History Month (WMC).

Olivia is faculty choreographer/teacher for Boy Blue, Laban Conservatoire and Greenwich Dance. She has also taught for various other companies across the UK and internationally.

Olivia looks forward to broadening her international presence as a performer and choreographer.
